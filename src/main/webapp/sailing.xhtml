Purpose of the Quality Indicator:

  • Measures the days that are good for a pleasurable sail on Betsie Bay and Lake Michigan.
Denominator Conditions:
  1. True if Observation was from the sailing season April - October.
Numerator Conditions:
True if all the conditions are satisfied.
  1. The Temperature must be greater the 50 degrees F.
  2. Winds speed must be between 3 and 35 knots.
I know there are many other points of data such as wave height, precipitation etc... However, this is only an example.
