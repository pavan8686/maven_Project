Purpose of the Quality Indicator:

  • Measures the days that are good for a Hang Gliding Empire Bluffs.
Denominator Conditions:
  1. True the observation station was Frankfort, Michigan (KFKS)
Numerator Conditions:
True if all the conditions are satisfied.
  1. Wind Speed between 20 and 40 mph
  2. Wind Direction between 70 & 110 degrees
I know there are many other points of data such as temperature, precipitation etc... However, this is only an example.
